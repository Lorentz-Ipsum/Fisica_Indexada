$$

$$
F_{\mu \nu} (A) = F_{\mu \nu} (A')
$$

$$
F_{\mu \nu} = - F_{\nu \mu}

## Acción EM

$$
S_{EM} = - \frac{1}{16\pi c} \int dx^4 F_{\mu \nu} (x)F^{\mu \nu} (x)
$$

$$
\delta S_{EM} = 0 \Leftrightarrow \mathbf{A}^{\mu}(\mathbf{x}) \rightarrow \mathbf{A}^{\mu}(\mathbf{x}) + \delta \mathbf{A}^{\mu}(\mathbf{x})
$$
